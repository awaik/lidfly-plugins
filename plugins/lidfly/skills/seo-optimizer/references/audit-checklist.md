# SEO And GEO Audit Checklist

Use this checklist after obtaining the page content and any available live data.

## Evidence

- Record URL, region, target audience, business goal, and audit date.
- Separate page observations, provider data, and hypotheses.
- Do not infer traffic, rankings, indexing, or conversions without a source.

## Technical And Indexing

- HTTP/indexability/canonical/robots/sitemap status when available.
- Yandex Webmaster diagnostics, indexed pages, query data, and crawl issues.
- Mobile readability, performance risks, broken links, and structured data visible in the supplied page or tools.

## Intent And Content

- Search intent and expected page type.
- Title, description, H1, heading coverage, first-screen promise.
- Missing subquestions, examples, proof, FAQ, and internal links.
- Duplicate or cannibalizing pages.
- Unsupported claims, stale facts, and user-provided business or legal constraints.

## GEO And AI Visibility

- Clear entity, service, geography, authorship, and contact facts.
- Quotable definitions, concise answers, comparison criteria, and sourceable claims.
- Consistent organization/product naming and relevant structured data.
- Do not promise inclusion in AI answers or search results.

## Conversion

- One clear primary action.
- Trust elements, objections, price/terms clarity, and form friction.
- Keep SEO recommendations separate from conversion hypotheses when no analytics is available.

## Priority Format

```markdown
| Priority | Finding | Evidence | Recommended change | Expected effect | Confidence | Effort |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
```

Put blockers first, then intent/content gaps, then enhancements. End with a short measurement plan.
